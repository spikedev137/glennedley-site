Push-up and Pull-up Ladders 3x per week

Usually combined with Hindu Squats and Push-ups as a circuit. Each round increases difficulty:

  • 10 Hindu Squats
  • 1 Pull-up
  • 1 Push-up
  • 10 putts
  • 5–10 elastic band pulls (with good form)

Then rest 30s and increase reps each round. Keep going until your form breaks.I add burpees, shoulder presses and other exercises, but this is my baseline.

Challenges

Are a great way to get into the habit of doing something when you said you would do it. For example rebounding started with Rebounding for One Minute a Day for 30 Days. As I write this I'm at Day 67. It stuck. Anyone can do something for a minute a day. Find a challenge, track with an app like Way of Life and you might be shocked what you're doing in a year
